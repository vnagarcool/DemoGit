2. Printer
--------------------------------------------

to print the data, when we print it it is providing an output.

1. impact printers:

	it printd characters by striking them on ribbon and then it is pressed on paper.
	
	Features:
		-->very noisy
		-->it requires physical contact on paper to print image
		-->very low consumable cost
		-->usefull for bulk printing


	2 types

	1. character printer
		prints one character at a time.
		examples. DMP (Dot Matrix Printer) , Daisy Wheel
	2. Line printers:
		print one line at time
		examples: Drum Printer, Chain Printer
				

2. Non- impact Printers:

	prints the characters on paper but without any ribbon. It prints full page at a time. thats why its is also called as a page printer.

	1. Laser Printers:
		uses laser lights to produce dots needed to form the characters to be printed on a page.
		 very high in speed, high quality, supports diffrent graphics and fonts

	2. InkJet Printers:
		this is based on new technology
		it print characteres by sprraying small dot  ink on paper and produce high quality output.
